    Trips in Nepal:

    Annapurna Base Camp



    Annapurna region is famous and most popular trekking destination of Nepal after Everest trek. Among them The Annapurna Base Camp trek is one of the most popular treks in the Annapurna region. Mt. Annapurna (8091m) of Nepal is the 10th highest mountain in the world and 8th highest peak of Nepal. This is short trek relatively easy and moderate trek not far from Pokhara.

    The altitude of Annapurna Base Camp is 4130 m. It is short and enthusiastic trek of Nepal. It is considered to be easy and moderate level trekking with cultural and natural tours. The entire route is beaded with the perfect views of beautiful Himalayan ranges, specific combination of rich cultural heritages.



    Duration: 11 days
    Trip Difficulty: Moderate
    Altitude: 827m to 4,130m
    Best Travel Months: March, April, May, September, October, November





    Day 1: Arrival in Kathmandu

    Explore the city and different Heritage site inside Kathmandu valley. You can do shopping and buy your necessary trekking gears near the city.
    Altitude: 1345m



    Day 2: Flight to Pokhara

    You will have short flight from Kathmandu to Pokhara and have lunch in Pokhara. After lunch you will do sightseeing in Pokhara such as Mahendra cave, Devis fall, Guptshwor Cave, International Museum, Peace Stupa, Boating in Fewa lake and street visit. It depends on your time consummation. For extra activities you can stay few days in Pokhara.
    Altitude: 827m/2700ft
    Flight Duration: 25 minutes



    Day 3: Trek to Ghandruk

    From Pokhara we have short drive to Naya Pul about 1 hour 30 minutes. From Birethanti we trek to Ghandruk, a beautiful Gurung village.
    Altitude: 1940m/6360 ft
    Trekking Hours: Approximately 5/6 hours
    Accommodation: Teahouse



    Day 4: Trek to Chhomrong

    This trail consists both ascending and descending. Through the trail we can see the beautiful view of Annapurna South, Hiunchuli, Machhapuchhre and Ganggapurna. After walking about 6 hours we will reach Chhomrong it is a beautiful village located on the lap of the Annapurna.
    Altitude: 2170m/7120ft
    Trekking Hours: Approximately 5/6 hours
    Accommodation: Teahouse



    Day 5: Trek to Bamboo

    In the beginning our trail goes down till Chhomrong Khola and ascend up to Sinuwa then our trail goes through the beautiful forest.
    Altitude: 2310m/7578ft
    Trekking Hours: Approximately 4/5 hours
    Accommodation: Teahouse



    Day 6: Trek to Deurali

    Our trail passes through the bamboo forest. Its short trail within 4 hours walk we will reach Deurali.
    Altitude: 3230m/10595ft
    Trekking Hours: Approximately 3/4 hours
    Accommodation: Teahouse



    Day 7: Trek to Annapurna Base Camp

    Our trail passes through the bank of the river. Our trail passes through Machhapuchhre Base Camp (3700m/12135ft). From MBC we can see the beautiful view of Mt. Machhapuchre, with Mt. Hiunchuli, Annapurna South, Annapurna I, Annapurna III, Gandharvachuli and Gangapurna. From MBC our trail goes ascending after short walk of 2 hours we can reach ABC. After reaching ABC our trek is completed, we are lucky to see the world 10th and Nepal 8th highest peak in front of us.
    Altitude: 4,130m/13,545ft
    Trekking Hours: Approximately 5/6 hours
    Accommodation: Teahouse



    Day 8: Trek Back to Bamboo

    After our breakfast we will leave Annapurna base camp and trek back Bamboo. Our trail goes descending downhill to Bamboo.
    Altitude: 2310m/7578ft
    Trekking Hours: Approximately 7/8 hours
    Accommodation: Teahouse



    Day 9: Trek to Jhinu Dand

    Our trail goes uphill and downhill till Jhinu Danda. In the beginning we ascend up to Kuldighar and descend down to Chhomrong Khola after rest we ascend to Chhomrong and walk downhill to Jhinu Danda.
    Altitude: 1760m/5770ft
    Trekking Hours: Approximately 5/6 hours
    Accommodation: Teahouse



    Day 10: Trek to Nayapul and Drive to Pokhara

    Our trek starts from Jhinu Danda early in the morning. Through our trail we can see the beautiful landscape. It takes about six hours to reach Nayapul. Our trek will end at Naya Pul then we drive to Pokhara. During our stay in Pokhara we can do shopping visit around lake side, yoga retreat and many more.
    Altitude: 1070m/3510ft
    Trekking Hours: Approximately 5/6 hours
    Driving Hours: 1 hours
    Accommodation: Teahouse



    Day 11: Fly Back to Kathmandu

    We have short flight about 25 minutes from Pokhara to Kathmandu. After arriving Kathmandu Its free day for our guests, you can visit around city, if necessary, shopping, visit bars, night clubs and many more.



    Day 12: Flight Home

    One of our guides will meet you at your hotel and drive you to the airport for your return flight home.
